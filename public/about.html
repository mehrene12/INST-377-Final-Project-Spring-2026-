About CareerPath

A career planning tool built for students by students.

What is CareerPath?

CareerPath is a web application designed to help students explore career options, compare salaries, and research graduate school opportunities around the world. Built as a final project for INST 377 at the University of Maryland.

APIs Used

USAJobs API

Official US government job listings with real salary data sourced from data.usajobs.gov.

REST Countries API

Used to retrieve geographic coordinates for countries, enabling map pins to display on the Grad School page.

Supabase

Our own database for storing and retrieving saved career paths.

How to Use

1

Go to the Jobs page to search US government job listings by keyword.

2

Save interesting careers using the Save Career button on any job listing.

3

Go to Grad School to search by major and explore top universities around the world.

4

Return to Home to see your saved careers and salary comparisons.
